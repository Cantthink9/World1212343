Blinding Light

The Blinding Light is the first one to step on this white plane. The first person to step on this world was a child from a dimension. He had the power to go into other dimensions via portal . Going here was a happy mistake. With his power he decided to bring others like him. Founding this nation. With the help of some alternative friends they automated the process .To the point there were over a septillion people. Some other things came out as well , the three most important ones were Two Gods and a relative from an alternative version which ended up causing the Mince War. Sending the population back to only about 150 billion. It seemed like there was finally peace but due to the demand of War became incredibly controlling of people to the point 50 billion people came to make the nation of the Destroying Fire. Eventually there was a revolution turning the Light into a utopian place to live in. In this reformed land you can do pretty much what you want as long as you don't step out of line.
